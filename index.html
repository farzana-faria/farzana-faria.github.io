Teaching Philosophy

Hands-on Learning

I believe that science is best learned through active experimentation. My classroom is a laboratory where students are encouraged to discover scientific principles through hands-on activities.

Critical Thinking

Science education should foster analytical minds. I challenge my students with problems that require them to think critically, evaluate evidence, and develop reasoned conclusions.

Real-World Connections

I strive to connect scientific concepts to everyday life. When students see how science explains the world around them, their interest and understanding grow naturally.
